Coffee Selection

In a quiet corner of the city, Coffee Selection Cafe is a special place to enjoy a cup of quality coffee. Carrying a minimalist but warm concept, this cafe combines a comfortable atmosphere with the aroma of fresh coffee brewed from selected beans. Every cup served is the work of experienced baristas, who understand that each coffee bean has its own story.

The flagship menu is single origin Arabica coffee, brewed using various methods ranging from pour-over, espresso, to cold brew. Not only coffee, Aroma Kopi Cafe also provides a variety of delicious snacks that are perfect to accompany moments of relaxation or work. With an interior dominated by wood and natural light, this cafe is an ideal place for anyone who wants to enjoy coffee in a calm and warm atmosphere.
